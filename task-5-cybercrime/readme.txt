## Cybercrime Type Chosen
**UPI / Online Payment Fraud**

## Part A – Case Study Summary
The case study describes a realistic scenario where a first-year engineering student 
is tricked by a fraudster impersonating a bank customer care executive. The attacker 
uses social engineering, a screen-sharing app, and a fake UPI collect request to steal 
money from the victim's account.

## Part B – Prevention Checklist
A "Stay Safe Online" checklist with 12 actionable tips including:
- General online safety habits
- 2 UPI-specific safety tips
- Reporting channels
