## 🛡️ Task 5 – Cybercrime Awareness Case Study

For Task 5, I researched **UPI/Online Payment Fraud** — one of the most rapidly growing cybercrimes in India — and produced a case study and prevention checklist.

---

### 🔍 Key Learning

What surprised me most during my research was how convincingly fraudsters impersonate legitimate bank officials, government officers, or customer support executives. Victims are often well-educated individuals who simply acted under pressure or urgency — something the attackers deliberately create.  

I had always assumed that only people who were unfamiliar with technology fell for such scams, but the data clearly shows that college students and young professionals are among the most frequently targeted groups, largely because they transact digitally very often.

---

### 🔐 Personal Reflection

One habit I will personally change as a result of this research is to never respond to any incoming call or message that asks me to share an OTP, click a link, or install a remote access application — regardless of how official it sounds.  

I will also make it a point to verify any UPI payment request before approving it, especially if it arrives unexpectedly. I now keep the National Cyber Crime Helpline number **1930** saved in my phone for emergencies.
